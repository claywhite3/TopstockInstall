Step 1 of 2 — Removal
Remove the Rail
Follow these steps to safely remove the damaged rail.
1
Open Both Access Panels
One panel per battery. Open both before proceeding.
2
Unplug Both Batteries
Pinch the connector and pull straight out. Do both. Do not pull by the wires.
3
Hold Rail Firmly
Grip the rail with both hands. Press on the bottom of the adapter to release the clip.
4
Rotate Rail Up
Once the clip releases, rotate the front of the rail upward away from the shelf.
5
Lift and Remove
Lift the rail off the adapter and set aside. The adapter stays on the shelf.
