Step 2 — Adapter
Standard
Install the adapter on standard grocery or retail shelving.
Installation Steps
1
Tight to Shelf
Press the adapter firmly against the front edge of the shelf. No gap between the adapter and the shelf edge.
2
Flat on Shelf
The back of the adapter must lie completely flat on the shelf surface. Any tilt will cause the rail to sit at an angle.
3
Insert 4 Push-In Rivets
Press one rivet firmly into each of the four mounting holes until it clicks flush with the adapter surface. The adapter is now secured.
