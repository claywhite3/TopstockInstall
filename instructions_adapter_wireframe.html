Step 2 — Adapter
Wire Frame
Install the adapter on open wire grid shelving.
Installation Steps
1
Set Above Shelving Facing
Position the adapter above the front edge of the wire grid shelving.
2
Push Down Until Fully Seated
Press the adapter straight down onto the top of the wire shelf edge until it sits flush.
3
Click Onto Bottom of Shelf Edge
Push the lower section of the adapter inward until it clicks firmly onto the bottom of the shelf edge.
