Reset Instructions
Follow both steps in order
Step 1 Power Cycle
1. Remove the cap and battery.
2. Wait 30 seconds.
3. Re-insert the battery and replace the cap.
Wait for the green LED to flash before proceeding to Step 2.
Step 2 Pinhole Reset
Locate the small pinhole below the LED light on the left side of the camera.

Using a SIM ejector tool or paperclip, press and hold for 10 seconds until the light flashes rapidly.
Reconnecting…
Returning to system check to verify the fix.
