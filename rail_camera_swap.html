Removal
1
Open Both Access Panels
One panel per battery. Open both before proceeding.
2
Unplug Both Batteries
Pinch the connector and pull straight out. Do both. Do not pull by the wires.
3
Hold Rail Firmly
Grip the rail with both hands. Press on the bottom of the adapter to release the clip.
4
Rotate Rail Up
Once the clip releases, rotate the front of the rail upward away from the shelf.
5
Lift and Remove
Lift the rail off the adapter and set aside. The adapter stays on the shelf.
Installation
1
Plug in Both Batteries
Connect both Molex plugs firmly into their ports. The rail LEDs should flash briefly to confirm power.
2
Close the Battery Covers
Push both covers firmly into place until you hear a click.
3
Hold Rail — Camera Up
Hold the rail with the camera facing up, then place it on the front edge of the adapter.
4
Rotate Down
Rotate the rail downward until it sits flat against the shelf face.
5
Pinch & Click
Pinch the two side tabs and press until the rail clicks securely into place.
